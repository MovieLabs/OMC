definition
An asset that consists of a group of other assets. For example, a 3D model of a Production Prop has geometry, textures, materials, and so on. The components can be made by different specialists, and managed separately. The fact that an Asset is an Asset Group may not be known when the Asset is created. In the physical world, Set Dressing is a good example of an Asset Group. Each individual element is an Asset, and can be purchased, on loan, or taken from a Prop warehouse, and will have different tracking and budgetary need.
