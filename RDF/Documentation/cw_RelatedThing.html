definition
This is an umbrella concept to group the similar, but different, concepts of RelatedWork and BasedOnThis. A RelatedWork is a CreativeWork to which some other CreativeWork stands in some relation. A BasedOnThis is an entity that served as the basis for a CreativeWork, e.g., a novel that is the basis for a movie. Even though such entities are creative works in a more general sense, within this ontology the notion of a CreativeWork is limited to a movie, video, TV program, or similar "playable" media.
