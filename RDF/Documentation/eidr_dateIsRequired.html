Property 'date is required'

type
DatatypeProperty
domain
Series
or Season
label
date is required
range
boolean
definition
If true then all child records (Seasons or seasonless Episodes of a Series, Episodes of a Season) must specify a full date (xs:date) for the Release Date field. This is useful when the season-less episodes do not have a title as in a news program or soap opera. This is usually not present or false at the Series level.
